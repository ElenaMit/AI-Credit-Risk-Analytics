# DAX Measures Generation

## Prompt

Act as a Senior Power BI Developer and Business Intelligence Analyst.

Based on the provided banking dataset, generate optimized DAX measures for a Credit Risk Analytics dashboard.

The dashboard should include:

### Core KPIs

* Total Customers
* Total Loan Amount
* Average Loan Amount
* High Risk Customers
* High Risk Rate

### Portfolio Analysis

* Loan Amount by Purpose
* Loan Amount by Housing
* Average Loan Amount by Housing
* Average Loan Duration by Housing

### Risk Analysis

* High Risk Customers by Age Group
* High Risk Rate by Age Group
* High Risk Customers by Customer Profile

### Customer Segmentation

Create business-friendly customer segments based on:

* Age
* Loan Amount
* Loan Duration

Example segments:

* Young High Borrower
* Young Low Borrower
* Mature High Borrower
* Mature Low Borrower

For each measure:

1. Generate DAX code.
2. Explain the business purpose.
3. Recommend the most appropriate Power BI visualization.
4. Suggest executive-level insights that can be derived from the metric.

Output should follow Power BI best practices and use clear business terminology.
