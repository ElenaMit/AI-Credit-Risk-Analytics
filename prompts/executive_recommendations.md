# Executive Recommendations

## Prompt

Act as a Senior Banking Business Consultant.

Based on the findings from the credit risk portfolio analysis:

* Identify the key risk drivers
* Identify improvement opportunities
* Assess portfolio concentration risks
* Identify customer segments requiring additional attention

Generate the following sections:

### Executive Summary

Provide a concise management-level summary of the portfolio situation.

### Key Findings

List the most important business findings and risk observations.

### Recommended Actions

Provide at least 4 actionable business recommendations aimed at improving risk management and portfolio performance.

### Expected Business Impact

Explain how the proposed actions may improve:

- Credit risk management
- Portfolio diversification
- Customer segmentation effectiveness
- Lending decision quality
- Overall portfolio performance

The output should be written in a professional executive style suitable for senior management presentations and business reports.
